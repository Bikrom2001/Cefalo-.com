Who we are

Cefalo is a Norwegian company specializing in assisting Norwegian companies with long-term hires of full-time premium software developers from our offices in Dhaka, Bangladesh.

We have been in business since 2010 and employ over 150 people.

Dhaka is a city of 20 million people and has a booming technology industry. We have succeeded in hiring the best of Dhaka´s developers and pride ourselves in being selected as the best outsourcing provider in Bangladesh several years in a row.

We make sure our employees have the best terms in the industry and place great emphasis on well-being, job security, and personal development for our developers. In return, these measures provide us with unique access to the best talents, very low turnover, and high stability in our workforce.
